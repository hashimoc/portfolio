Projects: Skill Surfer

As part of a UI/UX Bootcamp I am currently enrolled in, I was tasked with creating an application that would aid in conntecting two user groups. I took lead as the Project Manager for a group and worked on creating a mentoring application where users can choose to be a mentor and/or learn skills from other people.

Team Information

For this project I was part of a four person team and served as the team's Project Manager. I was responsible for speciic tasks such as making sure my team was meeting deadlines and being the person my team would turn to if they needed any assistance during the course of the project.

Purpose

This project was done during the course of a UI/UX bootcamp affiliated with the University of California Irvine. It was the first group project assigned for this particular cohort.

Tools/Methods

Throughout this project my team took advantage of methods such as affinity diagramming, creating user personas, conducting user interviews, and creating a competitive analysis. We used platforms such as Miro, Canva, and Figma to aid us during our project.

Design Question

How might we aid learners in connecting with the support they need when acquiring new skills in order give them the best chance of success?
